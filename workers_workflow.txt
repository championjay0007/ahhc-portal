======================================================================
ALLEGANCE HEART & HOME CARE - WORKER / SUPPLIER OPERATION FLOW
======================================================================

IMPORTANT ACCESS RULES FOR WORKERS:
- Workers/suppliers must ONLY see the participant’s name, preferred name, address, mobile number, and shifts they are assigned to.
- They MUST NOT see full funding details, unrelated clinical documents, or management-only records.
- Workers only gain access to participant details AFTER AHHC officially assigns them.
- Immediate deactivation and removal from participant assignment occurs when no longer approved.


1. WORKER ONBOARDING & COMPLIANCE WORKFLOW
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Step 1: Invitation / Sourcing
- System records if the worker is sourced by AHHC, the participant directly, a marketplace (e.g., Mable), or another provider.
- AHHC sends an invite or participant nominates the worker for approval.

Step 2: Profile & Compliance Upload
- Worker uploads mandatory compliance documents: ABN, police check/worker screening, insurance, qualifications/registration, training, first aid/CPR, and marketplace agreements.

Step 3: AHHC Review & Expiry Tracking
- AHHC reviews and approves/rejects compliance documents.
- System tracks document expiry dates and triggers non-compliance alerts/reminders.

Step 4: Sign Declarations
- Worker electronically signs mandatory declarations: Privacy, incident/complaint reporting, care note requirements, and agreement not to commence work before approval.

Step 5: Service Approval & Participant Assignment
- AHHC approves the worker for specific service types.
- AHHC assigns the worker to a specific participant. (Worker dashboard remains empty of client data until this step).


2. DAILY SHIFT & CARE NOTE WORKFLOW
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Step 1: Login & Dashboard Check
- Worker logs in and views their dashboard: Today’s shifts, notes due, compliance status, and alerts.

Step 2: View Assigned Shifts
- Worker views scheduled/approved shifts, including service type, start/end time, location, and specific service instructions/risks relevant to safe delivery.

Step 3: Deliver Service & Submit Care Note
- Worker submits a care note strictly against their assigned participant/shift (auto-filled by the system; cannot select unrelated participants).
- Required Care Note Captures:
  * Service date/time (Start/finish time, total hours, category).
  * Tasks completed (Tick list and free text aligned to care plan).
  * Participant presentation (General wellbeing, changes, concerns).
  * Risks/incidents (Prompts for falls, missed services, escalations).
  * Evidence/attachments (Optional photo/receipt/timesheet if consented).

Step 4: Worker Declaration
- Worker digitally confirms the note is accurate and the service was delivered as stated.


3. INCIDENT & RISK REPORTING WORKFLOW
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Step 1: Identify Event
- During or after a shift, worker identifies an incident, near miss, hazard, or change in the participant's condition.

Step 2: Submit Digital Form
- Worker submits the specific form via the portal (Incident, Falls, Near Miss, Hazard/Risk, or Change in Condition).
- Form captures details like location, immediate action taken, injury status, and escalation.

Step 3: Immediate Alert Trigger
- System immediately triggers a notification to authorized AHHC care/quality/management staff.
- Worker follows up with any required photo evidence or witness details.


4. INVOICE SUBMISSION WORKFLOW (For Supplier Roles)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Step 1: Submit Invoice
- Supplier submits invoice/receipt, linking it to the specific participant, service date, and service category.

Step 2: Attach Evidence
- Attaches mandatory evidence: care notes, timesheets, quotes, or Mable booking agreements.

Step 3: Await AHHC Review
- Invoice status shows as "Submitted/Pending".
- AHHC checks budget availability, care plan alignment, and worker compliance before approving payment.


5. WORKER DASHBOARD OPERATIONS & MENUS
----------------------------------------------------------------------
- My Dashboard: Snapshot of assigned shifts, notes due, compliance status, and alerts.
- Assigned Participants: View only assigned participants and approved limited service information.
- My Shifts: View scheduled/approved shifts, times, and locations.
- Submit Care Note: Log tasks, observations, and declarations for completed shifts.
- Incident/Risk Forms: Submit falls, hazards, injuries, or change-in-condition reports.
- Upload Documents: Upload/renew police checks, insurance, qualifications, and training evidence.
- Forms to Sign: Sign worker declarations, privacy agreements, and third-party worker agreements.
- My Profile: Manage contact details, ABN, service types, compliance expiry dates, and notification preferences.

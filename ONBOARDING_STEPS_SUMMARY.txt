# AHHC ONBOARDING STEPS SUMMARY

This document provides a simple summary of the onboarding process used in the portal.

## Participant Onboarding Steps
1. Enquiry Submitted
   - A person submits an enquiry or application through the public site.

2. Enquiry and Suitability Review
   - AHHC reviews the enquiry and checks whether the participant is suitable for the service model.

3. Participant Profile Created
   - If approved, AHHC creates the participant profile and records the initial support information.

4. Documents and Agreements
   - The participant uploads required documents and signs the required agreements.

5. Admin Review and Approval
   - AHHC administration reviews the submission and either approves it, requests updates, or rejects it.

6. Invitation and Portal Access
   - Once approved, the participant receives an invitation and can access the portal.

## Worker Onboarding Steps
1. Worker Nomination and Review
   - A participant may nominate a worker they wish to receive support from.
   - AHHC reviews the nomination before proceeding.

2. Invitation Sent
   - If the nomination is approved, AHHC sends the worker an onboarding invitation.

3. Account Setup
   - The worker creates their account and completes the required login setup.

4. Compliance Documents
   - The worker uploads required compliance and qualification documents.

5. Review and Declarations
   - AHHC reviews the submitted documents and the worker signs the required declarations.

6. Service Approval and Assignment
   - If approved, the worker is assigned to participants and can provide approved services.

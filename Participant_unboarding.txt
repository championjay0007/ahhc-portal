ALLEGIANCE HEART & HOME CARE (AHHC)
PARTICIPANT ONBOARDING PROCESS & WORKFLOW
Confidential Build Brief Extract
================================================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
The AHHC Self-Management Support portal operates on a strict access control 
principle. Users cannot self-create full portal accounts. All onboarding must 
go through an AHHC suitability review and approval process before a secure 
portal invitation is issued. 

This document outlines the mandatory 6-step onboarding workflow for new 
participants and their nominated support persons.

================================================================================
CLIENT ONBOARDING WORKFLOW (6 STEPS)
================================================================================

STEP 1: ENQUIRY / APPLICATION
-----------------------------
- Trigger: A prospective participant or their representative visits the public 
  AHHC website (allegiancehearthomecare.com.au/self-management-support).
- Action: The user completes the public Contact/Enquiry Form (capturing name, 
  email, phone, role, and Support at Home status).
- System Behavior: The form submission is routed to AHHC admin. 
- Security Note: No full portal access is created at this stage. It simply 
  generates an enquiry for AHHC review.

STEP 2: SUITABILITY ASSESSMENT
------------------------------
- Trigger: AHHC receives the initial enquiry.
- Action: AHHC conducts a suitability assessment to determine if the 
  participant is appropriate for the self-management support model.
- System Behavior: AHHC staff can either upload a completed suitability 
  assessment document to the system or assign an assessment form to the 
  prospective participant for completion.

STEP 3: PARTICIPANT PROFILE SETUP
---------------------------------
- Trigger: Suitability assessment is approved.
- Action: AHHC Admin/Care Manager creates the foundational participant record 
  in the secure portal.
- Data Captured: 
  * Support plan and Care plan
  * Quarterly budget and quarter dates
  * Emergency contacts and communication preferences
  * Support person authority levels (if applicable)
  * Identified risks

STEP 4: DOCUMENT UPLOAD
-----------------------
- Trigger: Profile is created.
- Action: Required documentation is gathered and securely uploaded to the 
  participant's file.
- Documents Required:
  * Referral / Support plan
  * Authority documents (e.g., proof of representation)
  * Service agreements
  * Participant handbook acknowledgement
  * Other relevant clinical or administrative records
- Security Note: All uploads are subject to file type controls, size limits, 
  and malware scanning.

STEP 5: ELECTRONIC SIGNING (E-SIGN)
-----------------------------------
- Trigger: Documents are uploaded and ready for execution.
- Action: AHHC assigns mandatory legal and consent forms to the participant 
  and/or support person via the portal's e-Signature module.
- Forms Required:
  * Self-Management Agreement
  * Consent and Privacy documents
  * Roles and Responsibilities acknowledgements
- System Behavior: The system tracks pending signatures and stores the final 
  signed PDF copies with a full audit trail (signer name, role, date/time).

STEP 6: DASHBOARD ACTIVATION & INVITATION
-----------------------------------------
- Trigger: All documents are signed, and AHHC grants final approval.
- Action: The system generates a secure portal invitation.
- System Behavior: 
  * The participant/support person receives a secure login invitation link 
    via email/SMS.
  * The user sets up their password and configures Multi-Factor 
    Authentication (MFA/2FA).
  * The user is granted role-based access to the Participant Dashboard.

================================================================================
POST-ONBOARDING: PARTICIPANT DASHBOARD CAPABILITIES
================================================================================
Once onboarded and activated, the participant/support person can securely log 
in to the mobile-responsive PWA/Web Portal to:

1. View Live Budget: Track quarterly budget, committed funds, approved/paid 
   spend, and remaining balance.
2. Manage Workers/Suppliers: Nominate and onboard their own chosen support 
   workers or third-party suppliers (subject to AHHC compliance approval).
3. Submit Pre-Approvals: Request new services, attach quotes, and select 
   service categories.
4. Submit Invoices: Upload receipts/invoices and link them to pre-approvals 
   or care notes.
5. Manage Documents: Upload and view care plans, agreements, and receipts.
6. Sign Forms: Complete any ongoing digital forms required by AHHC.
7. Report Incidents/Feedback: Submit complaints, feedback, or incident reports.
8. Secure Messaging: Communicate securely with the AHHC care team.
